What is Shellshock?

Shellshock! Take control of your own personal snail in this active battle arena. Climb walls, jump and stick to ceilings just like a real snail! Use sodium based weapons to bring down the competition. Use gravity to your advantage, shooting your Beezooka in impressive arcs, but watch out, with high damage weapons your action points will be used up faster and leave you unable to defend yourself.

This game is suitable for users 15 and up.
